Odin Recipe: Lasgana

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings

  1. 3 cups ricotta cheese
  2. 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  3. 2 egg
  4. 1 pound ground beef
  5. 1 jar (45 ounces) Prego® Traditional Italian Sauce or two jars (24 ounces each) Prego® Three Cheese Italian Sauce
  6. 12 cooked lasagna noodles, drained
  7. 12 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese (about 3 cups)
